Our Story

Big flavour, born in East Ham.

Tucked into the buzz of High Street South, Ashes is a bustling burger restaurant with a whole lot more on the grill.

From gourmet burgers and wraps to loaded chicken fries and American-style hot dogs, we cater to every kind of appetite. Regulars come back for the Custom Pizza, the robust Half-Pounder Burger and our Premium Nacho Fries — with a side of French fries never far behind.

We're at our liveliest in the evening, when the flame's at its hottest and the place is full — exactly how a neighbourhood grill should feel.
